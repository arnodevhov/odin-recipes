Berry Smoothie

A refreshing and healthy smoothie bowl packed with berries, fruits, and crunchy toppings. Great for breakfast or a light snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional)

Steps

  1. Add banana, berries, yogurt, milk, and honey to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the smoothie into a bowl.
  4. Add toppings like granola, strawberries, blueberries, and chia seeds.
  5. Serve immediately.
